Optimistic Concurrency Control (OCC) is the core transaction model of Apache Iceberg. Writers perform operations in isolated staging spaces. Upon completion, they validate that files targeted for updates still exist and no concurrent modifications have advanced the table sequence. Finally, they commit the change atomically via a catalog pointer swap.
