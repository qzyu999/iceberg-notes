Milestone Integration Strategy
To establish principal-level architectural influence, contributors must ensure strict feature-level compliance across PyIceberg and Rust native extensions, ensuring that serialization, lock retry mechanics, and mathematical proof constraints are strictly respected.

Multi-language support is a fundamental pillar of the Apache Iceberg architecture. To prevent vendor lock-in and enable performant execution environments across Python (PyArrow) and Rust, all specification extensions must be ported and implemented across core bindings in exact lockstep alignment.
